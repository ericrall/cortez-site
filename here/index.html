Cortez Architecture Phase I Why Cortez

Your iPhone drives your Mac the way you would.

Even when accessibility is broken, when an app has no API, when no agent is installed on the Mac. Cortez looks at the screen through the iPhone's camera, and types and clicks over Bluetooth.

What Cortez does today

MailCompose, address, send.
SafariFill forms, navigate.
MessagesSend to a contact.
FinderOpen and navigate.
System SettingsAdjust panes.
SpotlightLaunch anything.
Cortez is a confidence-propagating perception-to-action pipeline with VoiceOver as the primary mediation layer. Every stage carries forward what it knows and what it isn't sure of, so the planner can decide instead of guess.
